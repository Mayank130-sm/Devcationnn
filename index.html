About Devcation

Where innovation meets collaboration in a week-long tech extravaganza

Devcation is a week-long technical festival organized by GDG IGDTUW, bringing together developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts from across the country.

With a series of workshops, hackathons, and networking events, Devcation aims to foster innovation and collaboration in the tech community.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, Devcation has something for everyone!

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Devcation is open to all students and professionals aged 18 and above. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, we welcome you to join! The only requirement is your passion for technology and innovation.

You can reach out to us on our Discord Server with any queries. We have dedicated channels for different types of questions. For urgent matters, you can also email us at contact@gdgigdtuw.com.

While coding experience is beneficial, it's not mandatory. We welcome participants with diverse skill sets including design, business development, and project management. We'll also have beginner-friendly workshops and mentors to help you get started.

For the hackathon, bring your laptop, charger, and any other devices you might need. For online participants, make sure you have a stable internet connection. We'll provide all necessary software details and resources once you register.
